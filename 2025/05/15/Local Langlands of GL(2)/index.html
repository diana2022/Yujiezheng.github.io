Local Langlands of GL(2)

Yujie Zheng
2025-05-15
Representation Theory

This semester, under the supervision of Professor Zhao Bin, I studied the Local Langlands Conjecture for GL(2) (specifically, Professor Zhao taught a dedicated course on this topic). This is my reading note, primarily structured based on the book by Colin J. Bushnell and Guy Henniart, supplemented with details I added. It is still being updated.
To assess student comprehension, Professor Zhao assigned supplementary readings. I was tasked with studying the appendix of Breuil, Christophe, and Ariane Mézard’s 2002 paper “Multiplicités modulaires et représentations de GL₂(ℤₚ) et de Gal(ℚ̄ₚ/ℚₚ) en ℓ=p” (Duke Mathematical Journal 115 (2): 205–310). This is my reading note for a late-June presentation. I’ve nearly completed it with only minor additions remaining.
